HR Employee Flow Analytics Dashboard

The organization faced challenges due to a lack of consolidated, real-time metrics on employee flow — leading to reactive staffing decisions and an inability to proactively address retention risks across departments and demographics.

Dataset Used
HR EMPLOYEE DATASET.xlsx — multi-departmental workforce records including employee demographics, tenure, hire/exit dates, department, age groups, salary, and gender classifications.

  • HR Department shows the highest relative attrition rate (29%), undermining internal recruitment efforts

  • Hiring trend volatility reveals inconsistent long-term workforce planning cycles

Recruitment Analytics · Looker Studio

Recruitment Pipeline & Efficiency Dashboard

Hiring teams lacked visibility into where candidates were dropping off, which sources delivered quality hires, and where delays were accumulating — making it impossible to improve candidate experience or reduce time-to-hire at scale.

Dataset Used
RECRUITMENT_DATASET.xlsx — end-to-end candidate records covering departments (Engineering, Sales, Marketing, HR, Finance), pipeline stages, sourcing channels (LinkedIn, Indeed, Referral, Careers Page), time-in-process, and hiring outcomes.
↗ Download Dataset
  • 50 total applications → 5 hires: average time-to-hire 10 days, 38 active candidates in pipeline
  • Referral and Careers Page candidates convert more efficiently than LinkedIn/Indeed high-volume sources
  • Technical Interview & Offer stages are the biggest bottlenecks (12–14 days average delay)
  • Engineering drives 42%+ of hiring demand with the longest close times across all departments
